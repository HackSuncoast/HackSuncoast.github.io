#HackSuncoast

Suncoast Community High School's Premier Hackathon

January 15th-17th, 8AM-3PM

About

#HackSuncoast is the Suncoast Community High School's Premier Hackathon where we aim to bring 11th and 12th grade Computer Science students together to design, code, create, and hack an idea around healthcare and how they can better connect their communities to healthcare service providers.

No prior experience as far as hackathons, wireframing, or even coding is necessary. They will learn new coding practices, be introduced to new languages & development tools, as well as be connected to mentors from the industry who will help cultivate their desires to learn more about various coding practices and bring their solutions to a tangible state.

This experience will allow them to see how enterprise developers and businesses come up with and create various applications and technological solutions that they implement within the business. This hands on experience will give them work in the real world of a programmer, and teach them concepts that they might not gain from a typical class lecture or assignment.

Why

In a world where technology is advancing, there is a huge disconnect between school technology curriculum and what is actually used and valued in the industry. Careers in Information Technology are not normally talked about or even acknowledged in day to day conversations, such as the frequent suggesting of careers as a professional athlete, lawyer, or doctor.

We’ve noticed that the STEM awareness in our local schools, but most importantly our High School, is slowly decreasing and needs a breath of fresh air and excitement to captivate the students’ interest in the magnet program. As it pertains to Suncoast High School in particular, there have been many changes to the Computer Science program that have caused students to become disinterested in the program and overlook the importance of the skills that they are actually gaining by being a part of this progressive, one of a kind magnet experience.

We want to bring the spark back to the Suncoast Computer Science program as we challenge the students who have a passion in cultivating technology to think outside the box. We want to show these students that they are in a program that is truly unique that can equip them with the tools necessary to have prosperous careers in IT.
