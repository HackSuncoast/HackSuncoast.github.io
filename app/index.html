#HackSuncoast

Suncoast Community High School's Premier Hackathon

January 16th-17th, 7:30AM-2:40PM

About

#HackSuncoast is the Suncoast Community High School's Premier Hackathon where we aim to bring 11th and 12th grade students together to design, code, create, and hack an idea around healthcare and how they can better connect their communities to healthcare service providers.

No prior experience as far as hackathons, wireframing, or even coding is necessary. They will learn new coding practices, be introduced to new languages & development tools, as well as be connected to mentors from the industry who will help cultivate their desires to learn more about various coding practices and bring their solutions to a tangible state.

This experience will allow them to see how enterprise developers and businesses come up with and create various applications and technological solutions that they implement within the business. This hands on experience will give them work in the real world of a programmer, and teach them concepts that they might not gain from a typical class lecture or assignment.

Schedule

Day 1

Time Planned Activity Description
7:50 AM Opening Ceremony / Introduction The program team and MC will do introductions and discuss the purpose behind the event.
8:10 AM Team Creation Upon the last introduction the MC will allow the students to break for team selection. Teams of 5 will break into their brainstorming session.
8:15 AM What are we trying to solve? The teams will find out what problem they are tasked with solving.
8:20 AM Mentor Introductions Mentors will be placed throughout the auditorium and the students will have the opportunity to draft their team’s mentor who will assist them throughout the process.
8:25 AM Let The Games Begin! Development begins.
10:30 AM Developer Talk Students will take a short break from developing. The break will include a hands on dev talk where a current industry developer will teach the students how to use "Git", which is an industry tool vital to a development team.
10:50 AM Off to the Races Development begins again.
11:45 AM Chat and Chew Students will have the opportunity to eat and talk to professionals in the STEAM field and notable Suncoast Alumni.
12:30 PM Get Back to Work Teams will continue to develop their solutions.
1:40 PM 1st Break / TED Talk #1 The Art of Failure (8 minute TED Talk) will be shown and a short discussion will follow.
1:50 PM Resume Work The students will resume working on their projects.
2:30 PM Students Pack Up The students will pack up and get ready for their dismissal.

Day 2

Time Planned Activity Description
7:40 AM Hacking Begins Teams get back to work with their mentors while a light breakfast is served.
9:05 AM Break / Activity Health related activity occurs, something light to get the blood pumping and to keep the students engaged.
9:10 AM Developer Talk Students will take a short break from developing. The break will include a hands on dev talk where a current industry developer will teach the students how to create a "Hello World" application in Node.js.
9:30 AM Get Back to Work Work resumes while snacks are brought out.
10:30 AM Developer Talk Students will take a short break from developing. The break will include a hands on dev talk where a current industry developer will teach the students how to create a "Hello World" program in the Python programming language.
10:45 AM Resume Work The students will resume working on their projects.
11:25 AM Working Lunches Lunch is brought out and students will have working lunches with their mentors as they wrap up their presentations.
12:30 PM Final Presentation Preparation Teams will finish their projects from the past two days and get ready to present their solutions.
12:45 PM Presentations Begin Each student group will have 3-5 minutes to present their POC that they’ve created.
2:05 PM Judges Deliberation As the judges deliberate on the awards, the #HackSuncoast Team will have a recap assembly with the students, answer questions, and conduct surveys.
2:30 PM Awards Ceremony After hard deliberation, the judges will announce the winners in the various categories and hand out awards.
2:40 PM Dismissal
